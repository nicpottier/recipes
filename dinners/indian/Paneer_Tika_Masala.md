# Panner Tika Masala

From: Jen Newcomer

Marinade
---------

* 1 cup yogurt
* 1 tbsp lemon juice
* 1 tsp cumin
* 1 tsp cinnamon
* 1 tsp cayenne
* 1 tsp ginger
* 3-4 chicken breasts

Sauce
-------
* 1 tbsp butter
* 6 garlic cloves, minced
* 2 tsp cumin
* 2 tsp paprika
* 8 oz tomato sauce
* 1 cup cream
* 1/4 cup corriander, chopped

Method
-------

Melt butter on med and saute garlic 1 min. Add spices, tomato suace and cream. Simmer on low for 20 mins. Add chicken and simmer for anotehr 10 min. Alternatively broil chicken, then add to sauce.
